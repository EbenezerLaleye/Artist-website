What is F.O.H.P?

The annual gospel concert is a highly-anticipated event that brings together a diverse group of gospel singers and musicians from around the country. The concert, which typically takes place in the summer, features a mix of traditional and contemporary gospel music, as well as performances from choirs, solo artists, and bands. The event is open to people of all ages and backgrounds, and attracts a large and enthusiastic crowd.
